About Us

Welcome to Mi Guagua, a family-run baby essentials store run by two devoted parents. Our name, “Mi Guagua”, means “my baby” in Chile heartfelt tribute to the love and care we put into everything we do. Our logo—featuring a loving mother holding her baby—perfectly captures what we stand for: warmth, security, and trust.
